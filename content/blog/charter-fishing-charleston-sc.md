Coastal cities all over the country have [charter fishing](/) services available for locals and tourists, but you’ll only find the best charter fishing here in [Charleston, SC](http://www.charlestoncvb.com/). With our beautiful beaches, perfect temperatures, and variety of species of fish to catch, Charleston is the perfect place for fishing. If you’re looking for the experience of a lifetime, book a fishing charter with Fish the Wahoo.![12 Hour Deep Sea Fishing Charter](/blog-images/12-Hour-Deep-Sea-Fishing-Fishing-Charter-225x300.jpg)

# Fish Species in Charleston

When you’re aboard [The Wahoo](/tour-boats/), one of the most important things you’ll want to know is what kind of fish you might catch. Some of the popular fish species we catch are redfish, sea trout, flounder, black drum, sheepshead, wahoo, tuna, snapper, grouper, marlin, bass, and even sharks! At the end of your trip, you’ll even get to keep some of the fish you caught! There’s nothing like spending a whole day (or night) out on the water and returning home with a reward for all of your hard work.
