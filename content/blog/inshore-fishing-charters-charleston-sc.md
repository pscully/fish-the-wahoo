# Inshore Fishing Charters

Our Inshore Fishing Charters are perfect for people who don’t have time for a long, 12 hour, offshore fishing trip. With the option of booking either a 4-hour or 6-hour trip, you get a chance to fish off of a brand new 24’ center console. Whether you’re a fishing fanatic in Charleston, or just learning the ropes, we have the perfect tour for you! Here are some other things you can expect from your trip:

**![Inshore Fishing Charters](/blog-images/Inshore-Fishing-Charter-225x300.jpg "Inshore Fishing Charter")**

-   **Easy Booking.** When you want to book your trip, our website is the easiest place to secure your spot on a boat. You simply have to choose what day you want your trip to take place, and how many people will be with you. For our inshore fishing charters, the maximum group size is 6 guests.
-   **Targeting Certain Fish Species.** During this trip, we will target such fish species as [Redfish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Redfish), [Sheepshead](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Archosargus_probatocephalus), [Trout](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trout), [Spadefish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlantic_spadefish), [Black Sea Bass](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_sea_bass), [Cobia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cobia), and [Shark](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shark). These species are commonly found in the Charleston area.
-   **Video Service.** While you’re on the boat, we will be filming using a drone, dredge, and underwater equipment to immortalize your trip in a breath-taking high-definition digital video! Now, you won’t have to tell your friends about your trip, you can show them!
-   **World Class Captains.** All of our captains are Tour Series Winning Captains are dedicated to optimizing your catch and your experience.
