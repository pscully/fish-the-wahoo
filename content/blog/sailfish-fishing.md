# Always Bridle While Sailfish Fishing

You need to bridle even the smallest live bait instead of just hooking the _bait’s dorsal fin_ or the nostrils drops many fish. Bridling creates a gap between the bait and the circle hook so when you’re turning and the catching the fish, the bridle prevents the hook from turning back into the bait. For small _kite baits_, use nostril bridle. Dorsal fin bridle will work more for the large kite baits. The core to _baiting sailfish_ is to use the smallest and light hook as much as possible because sailfish are ideal light-tackle fish.
