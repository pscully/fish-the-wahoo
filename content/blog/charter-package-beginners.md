Ocean fishing is one of the most exciting experiences you can offer your kids and friends who haven’t had the opportunity to land big salt water fish. A full day of fishing from even a well-appointed yacht like the Wahoo can be tiring though, especially for kids. An Inshore fishing charter package is the best of both worlds- you can catch some of the exciting fighting sport fish without the expense and commitment of a full day trip.

## Inshore Fishing Charter Package

For beginners new to fishing and families with children, an inshore fishing charter package is the idea day on the water. You’ll have plenty of opportunities to get your lines wet from our 23 foot center console style boat and head out. The captain and crew will take you to a spot where we believe the fish will be biting. If the fish aren’t cooperative, we’ll simply move on to a new spot. With inshore fishing, nothing is guaranteed but a nice day on the water. It’s all about the experience, getting out on the boat, enjoying the sun and company in a laid-back, relaxed atmosphere.

## Family Fun in the Sun

Looking for a wider variety of experiences? Check out our Family Fun in the Sun charter. Charleston is a big place, and it’s hard to get to all the spots you want to visit if you have to make the trek on land from beach to beach. Besides, there’s no fishing from a car! With our Fun in the Sun package, your family can visit several beaches aboard our 23 foot center console boat, Three Dog Night. We offer a professional guide to take you to a private island where you can land on the beach and spend the day basking on the sand, watching dolphins, or we can head out to explore the back waterways, [Sullivan’s Island](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sullivan%27s_Island,_South_Carolina), Isle of Palms, and more. Whether you’re looking for a family day out or a day of bar hopping, we have an option for you.

When you’re ready to try out inshore fishing or cruising, call [Charleston Fishing Charters](/) and fish the Wahoo! Call for your date today.
