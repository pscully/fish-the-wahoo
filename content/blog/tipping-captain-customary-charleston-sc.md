# Tips on Tipping The Captain On Your Fishing Trip

Because we have world class captains and are offering you an incredible fishing trip, whether it be deep sea, off shore, or near shore, tipping The Captain is generally customary. Your [captain and crew work hard](http://www.thesailingclub.org/forms/Skippercrew.PDF) to ensure you have a good time while out on the water, so reward them with a good tip! The industry tipping standard is usually 20% of your total package cost, but the crew will be happy to accept any amount, as long as you think it accurately reflects what they deserve.
