## Why Are Deep Sea Fishing Charters So Popular?

Fishing enthusiasts love being out on the water, and here at Charleston Fishing Charters, we make your trip all the more enjoyable. We offer two basic ways to fish while you are out on the water with us – trolling fishing and bottom fishing. Trolling fishing is done on top of the water and is the way to fish for the big catches like sailfish, mahi, marlin, wahoo, and tuna, along with other similar surface feeding fish. Bottom fishing involves dropping anchor and sending a line down to the bottom in order to fish for triggerfish, grouper, sea bass, snapper, and other bottom dwellers.

Trolling will often not yield as many fish but you can attach bigger fish and enjoy a more exciting and thrilling experience. Bottom fishing will have you catching a lot of fish, including some that can put up a thrilling challenge to bring up to the surface at times. The full day sea fishing charter package gives you time to do both and catch a wide range of fish to fill your coolers to take home and enjoy. We do need to remind our potential guests that there is a six-passenger maximum set for all of our charter boats due to Coast Guard regulations, so please take that into account when booking your Charleston harbor cruise and fishing trip.
