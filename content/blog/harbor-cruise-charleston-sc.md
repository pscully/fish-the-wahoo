Charleston is one of the most popular destinations for history buffs. The charm of the area is in the stories that make up the very fabric of United States history, many of which happened right here. What better way to explore some of Charleston’s waterfront history and architecture than taking a luxurious harbor cruise? Aboard the Wahoo, guests will relax on as we head out from the Ripley Light Marina, passing the Ashley Marina, the City Megadock, the Sailboat Graveyard, and more. We’ll meander along to Fort Sumter, or perhaps Fort Moultrie, The Jetties, or to one of Charleston’s historic lighthouses. No matter where we go, we can promise you will love the Wahoo harbor cruise!

# Where Will We Go on our Harbor Cruise?

## ![harbor-cruise-charleston-sc](/blog-images/boatpic3-300x138.png)

We’ll make our way along to the [Ravenel Bridge](http://www.sciway.net/sc-photos/charleston-county/ravenel-bridge.html), and view more of Charleston’s attractions before heading back to the arena. There’s so much to see in Charleston, and our harbor cruise captains are well versed in the history and stories relating to each and every historic place. Depending upon the interests of the guests, the Captain will tailor your tour to what you’d most like to see and experience, and will make brief stops to explain the significance of points of interest. You can’t beat a harbor cruise that is customized to you!

To round out the tour, we offer catering with our executive chef. Light or heavy hors d’oeuvres, or entrees are available. Chef Michaels specializes in international cuisine, including French, Italian, American, Japanese, Cajun and Thai. Contact Robb to discuss the options and to plan your event.
