With the beautiful beaches, perfect temperatures, and variety of species of fish to catch, Charleston is the perfect place for fishing.

When you’re on your fishing charter, one of the most important things you’ll want to know is what kind of fish you might catch. Some of the popular fish we catch include Blue Marlin, Sailfish, Wahoo, Mahi Mahi, and Tuna. At the end of your trip, you’ll even get to keep some of the fish you caught! There’s nothing like spending a whole day out on the water and returning home with a reward for all of your hard work.

# Fishing Locations

Charleston is a gold mine of various creeks, rivers, marshes, and other inshore and offshore areas. The Charleston Harbor, Isle of Palms, Sullivan’s Island, James Island: all of these places offer new opportunities for inshore fishing. As for offshore fishing, taking a fishing charter off the coast of Charleston is the best way to fully immerse yourself in the fishing experience. Whether you are a seasoned angler or a first timer, charter fishing in Charleston is sure to be an unforgettable experience.
