# Why Charter Fishing Is The Best In Charleston, SC

Coastal cities all over the country have charter fishing services available for locals and tourists, but you’ll only find the best charter fishing here in Charleston. With our beautiful beaches, perfect temperatures, and variety of species of fish to catch, Charleston is the perfect place for fishing. If you’re looking for the experience of a lifetime, book a fishing charter with Fish the Wahoo.![Charter-Fishing-Charelston](/blog-images/Charter-Fishing-Charelston-300x187.jpg)

# Fish Species in Charleston

When you’re on your fishing charter, one of the most important things you’ll want to know is what kind of fish you might catch. Some of the popular fish species we catch are redfish, sea trout, flounder, black drum, sheepshead, wahoo, tuna, snapper, grouper, marlin, bass, and even sharks! At the end of your trip, you’ll even get to keep some of the fish you caught! There’s nothing like spending a whole day (or night) out on the water and returning home with a reward for all of your hard work.

# Fishing Locations

Charleston is a gold mine of various creeks, rivers, marshes, and other inshore and offshore areas. The Charleston Harbor, Isle of Palms, Sullivan’s Island, James Island: all of these places offer new opportunities for inshore fishing. As for offshore fishing, taking a fishing charter off the coast of [Charleston](http://www.charleston-sc.gov/) is the best way to fully immerse yourself in the fishing experience. Whether you are a seasoned angler or a first timer, charter fishing in Charleston is sure to be an unforgettable experience.
